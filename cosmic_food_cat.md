# Cosmic Food Cat

The cosmic food cat branch is one of the most popular and immediately legible substyles in `laser_cat_style`.

It places cats in direct, absurd, and often throne-like relationship with food items, especially junk food, simple meme-legible foods, or household edible objects.

This file defines how that branch functions.

---

# Core principle

Food becomes:
- vehicle
- throne
- mount
- habitat
- celestial body
- absurd context
- visual punchline

The cat does not just eat the food.
The cat occupies, rides, merges with, or dominates the food.

This is not food photography.
This is food nonsense.

---

# Why food works so well

Food is powerful in this style because it is:
- instantly readable
- culturally loaded
- color-rich
- shape-distinct
- absurd when oversized
- absurd when used as furniture
- meme-native
- nostalgic
- lowbrow

A cat on a pizza is funny immediately.
A cat inside a slice of bread is iconic.
A cat floating with burger, fries, and milkshake is a complete visual system.

# Primary food families

## 1. Pizza

One of the strongest motifs.

Use cases:
- cat riding pizza
- cat on pizza slice
- pizza as raft or throne
- pizza as celestial object

Strengths:
- circular shape
- color contrast
- meme recognition
- easy to scale absurdly

Risks:
- becoming generic unless the cat or background adds more

---

Classic food-cat mount.

Use cases:
- cat on burger
- burger as throne
- floating burger with cat

Strengths:
- layered visual complexity
- color richness
- iconic junk food shape

# Food-cat composition modes

## Ride mode
The cat is actively riding or mounted on the food.

Best for:
- pizza
- burger
- taco
- shark + food combinations

## Throne mode
The cat sits on the food as if entitled to it.

Best for:
- sandwich
- burger
- pizza
- large food platform

## Merge mode
The cat is fused with, framed by, or inside the food.

Best for:
- bread
- donut
- sandwich
- taco shell

## Scatter mode
Multiple food items float around the cat.

Best for:
- fries
- multiple burgers
- mixed food cloud
- cosmic junk food debris

---

# Scale behaviors

Food works well when:
- oversized against the cat
- equal to the cat
- floating like celestial bodies
- clustered in absurd density

Food fails when:
- realistically scaled and realistically placed
- too small to matter
- visually muddy
- generic without cat interaction

---

# Background pairings

Best backgrounds for food cat:
- galaxy fields
- black void stage
- bright gradients
- cosmic nebulae
- simple color fields

The contrast between:
- mundane food
- cosmic background
- absurd cat

is a major part of the joke.

---

# Color behavior

Food items provide:
- yellow (fries, cheese)
- red (tomato, pepperoni)
- brown (burger, bread)
- green (lettuce, garnish)
- white (milkshake, bun)

These colors should:
- pop against cosmic backgrounds
- remain readable
- support the cat's visual presence
- not become muddy or desaturated

# Common failure modes

## Generic food + cat
The image is just "cat near food" with no absurdity.

Fix:
- change the scale
- change the relationship
- add cosmic background
- add more objects
- make the food a throne or vehicle

## Too realistic
The food and cat look like they belong together.

Fix:
- increase scale absurdity
- add collage mismatch
- add cosmic field
- add floating logic
- add more nonsense objects

## Food dominance
The food is more memorable than the cat.

Fix:
- make the cat larger
- make the cat more expressive
- make the cat-occupation more central
- reduce food detail

## Under-escalated
The scene is too simple.

Fix:
- add more food items
- add cosmic debris
- add glam accessories
- add glow effects
- add co-star creatures
