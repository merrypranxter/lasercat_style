# Giant Cat Attack and God-Cat

The giant cat attack and god-cat branch is one of the most dramatic and visually powerful substyles in `laser_cat_style`.

It scales cats up to absurd, threatening, or divine proportions and places them over cities, landscapes, or cosmic scenes as forces of nature, judgment, or ridiculous power.

This file defines how that branch functions.

---

# Core principle

The cat becomes:
- kaiju
- deity
- destroyer
- cosmic judge
- fake apocalypse
- absurd authority

The scale is wrong.
The power is fake.
The majesty is stupid.

This is the style's most epic branch, and it depends on preserving the contradiction between:
- dramatic visual treatment
- cat idiocy

---

# Why giant cat works so well

Giant cat is powerful because it:
- creates instant absurdity through scale
- assigns fake divinity
- generates meme-native iconography
- supports laser/rainbow emission logic
- works with city and apocalypse backgrounds
- delivers fake-epic humor
- is immediately readable

A cat the size of a skyscraper is funny before anything else happens.

---

# Major giant cat modes

## 1. City-dominating cat

The cat looms over a city skyline.

Characteristics:
- cat larger than buildings
- city in foreground or midground
- cat as background threat
- dramatic scale contrast

Best for:
- monster/god-cat branch
- threat tableau mode
- laser eye scenes
- apocalypse humor

---

## 2. Beam-judging cat

The cat emits lasers or rainbow beams at the city or scene.

Characteristics:
- eye or mouth emissions
- directed energy
- destruction suggestion
- fake-apocalyptic energy

Best for:
- destroyer cat
- laser cat overlap
- graphic poster mode

---

## 3. Blank-eyed looming cat

The cat stares without visible emotion, creating ominous absurdity.

Characteristics:
- neutral or blank expression
- giant face or silhouette
- no clear emotional read
- fake-mythic presence

Best for:
- god-cat mode
- deity absurdity
- surreal threat

---

## 4. Action-attack cat

The cat is mid-motion, swatting, jumping, or destroying.

Characteristics:
- dynamic pose
- motion suggestion
- active destruction
- kinetic energy

Best for:
- kaiju-cat action
- adventure absurdity
- high-energy scenes

---

## 5. Silhouette god-cat

The cat is reduced to shape, creating iconic graphic power.

Characteristics:
- silhouette or near-silhouette
- strong shape readability
- minimal detail
- maximum symbolic impact

Best for:
- graphic poster mode
- Tumblr-adjacent collage
- strong contrast scenes

---

## 6. Floating cosmic cat

The cat hovers in space as a celestial body.

Characteristics:
- cosmic background
- cat as planet or star
- surreal scale
- space-native presence

Best for:
- space collage cat overlap
- cosmic deity mode
- abstract absurdity

---

# Scale behaviors

## Cat vs city
The cat dominates the urban environment.

## Cat vs landscape
The cat towers over natural scenery.

## Cat vs void
The cat floats in cosmic space as the main presence.

## Cat vs cat
Multiple giant cats create chorus or swarm absurdity.

Best backgrounds for giant cat:
- city skylines

The background provides:
- scale reference
- stakes
- drama
- contrast

Giant cat scenes often use:
- grayscale city with colorful cat
- black silhouette with rainbow beams
- cosmic color fields
- high contrast
- dramatic lighting

- pop against the background
- remain readable at huge scale
- maintain visual power
- not become muddy or lost

- blank or neutral expressions

Also viable:
- any cat that can handle scale without losing identity

---

# Common failure modes

## Too serious
The image becomes genuine kaiju or fantasy art.

Fix:
- add cat idiocy
- add absurd context
- add tacky elements
- reduce cinematic drama
- reintroduce humor

## Cat too small
The scale contrast is weak.

Fix:
- increase cat size
- reduce city detail
- emphasize cat dominance

## Cat too detailed
The giant cat becomes visually noisy.

Fix:
- simplify the cat
- use silhouette
- reduce texture
- increase graphic power

## Under-escalated
The scene lacks drama.

Fix:
- add beams
- add apocalypse effects
- increase scale
- add more giant cats
