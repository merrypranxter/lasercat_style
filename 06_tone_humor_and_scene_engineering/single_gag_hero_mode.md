# Single-Gag Hero Mode

Single-gag hero mode is one of the two primary composition modes in `laser_cat_style`.

It creates focused, iconic compositions where one clear premise carries the entire image.

This file defines how single-gag hero mode works.

---

# Core principle

In single-gag hero mode:
- one joke dominates
- the composition is simple
- the focal subject is clear
- the premise is immediate
- the silhouette is strong
- the readability is high

The joy comes from the clarity and iconicity of the single absurd premise.

---

# Why single-gag hero works

Single-gag hero is powerful because it:
- delivers instant readability
- creates strong meme power
- is highly shareable
- works at thumbnail size
- is easy to classify
- supports prompt systems
- is compositionally stable

A cat in bread is funny immediately.
A cat on pizza is iconic instantly.

---

# Typical structures

## 1. Object-merge hero

The cat is fused with or framed by one dominant object.

Examples:
- cat in bread
- cat wrapped in toilet paper
- cat in box
- cat on sandwich

Strengths:
- immediate joke
- strong silhouette
- iconic readability

---

## 2. Ride hero

The cat rides one dominant vehicle or mount.

Examples:
- cat riding pizza
- cat riding shark
- cat riding taco
- cat riding unicorn

Strengths:
- adventure absurdity
- strong focal unit
- narrative suggestion

---

## 3. Throne hero

The cat sits on one dominant platform.

Examples:
- cat on burger
- cat on sandwich throne
- cat on pizza platform

Strengths:
- entitlement humor
- stable composition
- iconic power

---

## 4. Emission hero

The cat generates one dominant effect.

Examples:
- cat with laser eyes
- cat with rainbow puke
- cat with glowing eyes

Strengths:
- visual intensity
- meme escalation
- strong focal point

---

## 5. Giant hero

The cat is scaled to dominate the scene.

Examples:
- giant cat face
- kaiju cat over city
- god-cat silhouette

Strengths:
- scale absurdity
- fake epic energy
- iconic power

---

# Composition rules

## One clear focal unit
The main subject must be immediately identifiable.

## Strong silhouette
The shape must read at distance.

## Minimal clutter
Support elements should not compete.

## Immediate joke
The premise must land in under one second.

## High contrast
The subject must pop from the background.

## Readable at small size
The image must work as thumbnail or icon.

---

# Background pairings

Best backgrounds for single-gag hero:
- galaxy fields
- black void stage
- bright gradients
- simple cosmic fields
- high-contrast scenes

The background should:
- support the hero subject
- provide contrast
- not compete for attention
- feel like a stage

---

# Color behavior

Single-gag hero scenes often have:
- focused color palette
- strong contrast
- clear subject-background separation
- minimal color clutter

The color should:
- support subject readability
- add visual punch
- not distract from the gag

---

# Tone behavior

Single-gag hero tends to produce:
- iconic clarity
- immediate humor
- meme-native power
- shareable simplicity
- low-stakes absurdity

The best examples feel:
- instantly legible
- compositionally clean
- gloriously stupid
- highly memorable

---

# Common failure modes

## Too simple
The gag is weak or under-escalated.

Fix:
- add effect
- add background drama
- increase scale absurdity
- add color intensity

## Too cluttered
Support elements compete with the hero.

Fix:
- reduce object count
- simplify background
- increase hero size
- improve focal hierarchy

## Unclear premise
The joke does not land immediately.

Fix:
- strengthen the cat-object relation
- simplify the composition
- make the premise more obvious
- add visual cues

## Weak silhouette
The subject does not read at distance.

Fix:
- simplify the shape
- increase contrast
- reduce detail
- strengthen the outline

**one clear absurd premise with iconic power and immediate readability**
