# Rainbow and Neon Logic

Rainbow and neon colors are essential to `laser_cat_style` identity.

## Rainbow logic

### Why rainbows work
- Instant recognition
- Cheerful absurdity
- Meme-native
- Color violence
- Tacky joy

### Rainbow applications
- Rainbow puke
- Rainbow lasers
- Rainbow gradients
- Rainbow auras
- Rainbow object accents

### Rainbow rules
- Bright, saturated bands
- Clear color separation
- Synthetic not natural
- Excessive not subtle

## Neon logic

### Why neon works
- Blacklight aesthetic
- Internet-native
- Synthetic intensity
- Night/cosmic association
- Tacky-cool tension

### Neon applications
- Glow effects
- Eye treatments
- Gradient backgrounds
- Edge highlights
- Aura effects

### Neon rules
- High saturation
- Synthetic colors
- Glow bleed
- Contrast with dark
- Excessive intensity

## Color combinations

### Classic neon pairs
- Pink/cyan
- Magenta/yellow
- Green/purple
- Orange/blue

### Rainbow sequences
- Full spectrum
- Partial spectrum
- Reversed spectrum
- Non-sequential

## Drift risks
- Pastel rainbows (too soft)
- Natural rainbows (too realistic)
- Subtle neon (too tasteful)
- Single color (not enough violence)
