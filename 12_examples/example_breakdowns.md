# Example Breakdowns

Detailed breakdowns of representative `laser_cat_style` images.

## Example 1: Classic Pizza Throne

### Description
A blank-faced orange tabby cat in loaf pose sits on a giant pizza slice, floating in a purple nebula with scattered stars.

### Breakdown
- **Cat**: Orange tabby, loaf pose, blank expression, medium scale
- **Object**: Pizza slice (throne), oversized
- **Background**: Purple nebula galaxy
- **Effects**: Subtle glow around cat
- **Composition**: Single-gag hero
- **Substyle**: Cosmic food cat
- **Cat role**: Throne-occupant
- **Humor engines**: Juxtaposition, scale absurdity
- **Tone**: Dumb delight

### Why it works
- Immediate joke clarity
- Strong silhouette
- Iconic premise
- Meme-native
