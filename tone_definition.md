# Tone Definition

`laser_cat_style` has a specific emotional and tonal register that is essential to its identity.

This file defines that tone and explains how it differs from adjacent styles.

---

# Core principle

The tone of `laser_cat_style` is:
- absurd
- stupid in the correct way
- campy
- low-stakes
- internet-native
- unserious
- colorful
- tacky
- affectionate
- ridiculous

It is not:
- solemn
- sincere
- tasteful
- elegant
- refined
- disciplined
- restrained
- high-art serious
- emotionally heavy

---

# The tone contradiction

The style often creates a productive tension between:
- dramatic visual treatment
- stupid subject matter

Examples:
- cosmic galaxy background + cat on pizza
- laser beams + blank-faced loaf cat
- apocalypse city + giant idiot cat
- neon glow + toilet paper cat

This contradiction is not accidental.
It is the engine.

The style wants:
- fake grandeur
- real stupidity

---

# Tone descriptors

## Absurd
The premise is unreasonable and immediately so.

## Campy
The style embraces tackiness, excess, and lowbrow pleasure without shame.

## Stupid (in the correct way)
The humor is direct, meme-native, and gloriously unpretentious.

## Colorful
Bright, synthetic, saturated color is part of the emotional delivery.

## Internet-native
The tone feels shaped by online culture, repost logic, and meme circulation.

## Affectionate
Even when mocking, the style loves its subject.

## Unserious
Nothing is at real stake. The apocalypse is funny.

# Tone families

## 1. Dumb delight

Pure joy in absurdity.

Examples:
- cat on pizza in galaxy
- rainbow puke cat
- floating food debris

Emotional effect:
- happiness
- amusement
- low-stakes pleasure

---

## 2. Fake epic

Dramatic treatment of stupid subject.

Examples:
- giant cat over city
- laser cat with beams
- god-cat silhouette

Emotional effect:
- mock awe
- absurd majesty
- ironic grandeur

---

The cat is simultaneously powerful and stupid.

Examples:

Emotional effect:

- power + incompetence humor

Emotional effect:

# Tone drift risks

## Too serious
The image becomes genuine fantasy or sci-fi art.

Fix:
- add absurd context
- add cat idiocy
- add tacky elements
- reduce cinematic drama

## Too cute
The image becomes kawaii without absurdity.

Fix:
- add stupid premise
- add cosmic escalation
- add object nonsense
- reduce cuteness discipline

## Too cool
The image becomes vaporwave or design-forward.

Fix:
- add goblin energy
- add food or household nonsense
- reduce graphic restraint
- increase tackiness

## Too random
The image has no coherent joke or logic.

Fix:
- establish clear premise
- strengthen cat centrality
- add recognizable motif
- improve composition hierarchy

---

# Tone and humor relationship

The tone enables the humor.

The style's humor works because the tone tells us:
- this is not serious
- this is not trying to be art
- this is celebrating stupidity
- this is internet-native
- this is for fun

Without that tone permission, the jokes would not land.

---

# Bottom line

`laser_cat_style` tone is:

**absurd, campy, stupid, colorful, internet-native, and affectionately ridiculous**

The style succeeds when it maintains the contradiction between:
- dramatic visual treatment
- gloriously dumb content

That contradiction is not a bug.
It is the feature.
