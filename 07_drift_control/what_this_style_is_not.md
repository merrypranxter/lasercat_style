# What This Style Is Not

Understanding what `laser_cat_style` is not helps prevent drift and clarify identity.

This file defines the boundaries of the style by describing adjacent but distinct aesthetics.

---

# Core principle

`laser_cat_style` has a specific identity.
It is not:
- everything with cats
- everything with rainbows
- everything cosmic
- everything collage
- everything funny

It is a specific combination of specific elements.

---

# Not generic space cat art

## What that is
Polished, cosmic cat imagery without meme-native absurdity.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` requires:
- collage logic
- absurd context
- object nonsense
- internet-trash energy

## Drift sign
The image looks like a fantasy poster or digital painting.

---

# Not generic trippy cat art

## What that is
Psychedelic cat imagery with sincere transcendence.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` requires:
- cat idiocy
- tacky execution
- meme-native humor
- internet-trash energy

## Drift sign
The image feels spiritually serious or decorative.

---

# Not generic meme collage

## What that is
Any funny image with multiple elements pasted together.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` requires:
- cat centrality
- specific motif families
- recognizable composition modes
- consistent tone

## Drift sign
The image could work with any animal or no animal.

---

# Not polished poster design

## What that is
Sleek, refined, commercially viable cat imagery.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` requires:
- collage visibility
- correct badness
- internet-trash energy
- tacky excess

## Drift sign
The image looks ready for a product line or gallery.

---

# Not kawaii cat illustration

Cute, soft, polished cat art.

- absurdity

- campy excess

# Not serious fantasy art

## What that is
Dramatic, world-built, narrative cat imagery.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` requires:
- stupidity
- internet-native humor
- collage logic
- low-stakes absurdity

## Drift sign
The image has coherent lore or serious stakes.

---

# Not vaporwave aesthetics

## What that is
Cool, nostalgic, design-forward digital art.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` requires:
- stupidity over coolness
- tackiness over restraint
- cat idiocy over graphic elegance

## Drift sign
The image feels stylish first and dumb second.

---

# Not Tumblr surreal collage

## What that is
Graphic, symbolic, fashion-aware surrealism.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` requires:
- cat-driven humor
- object nonsense
- internet-trash energy
- less symbolic, more literal absurdity

## Drift sign
The image could work with a sad girl, moon, and statue instead of a cat.

---

# Not stoner meme spam

## What that is
Lazy weed jokes with no broader style logic.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` requires:
- collage assembly
- color escalation
- cosmic background
- object scatter
- structural composition

## Drift sign
The image is just "cat + weed leaf" with nothing else.

---

# Not bad Photoshop humor

## What that is
Any poorly edited funny image.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` requires:
- recurring motif families
- stable composition modes
- recognizable tone
- consistent artifact preferences

## Drift sign
The image is technically bad but not stylistically patterned.

---

# Not Nyan Cat derivatives

## What that is
Specific pixel-art rainbow cat iconography.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` is:
- broader
- more collage-based
- more found-image-driven
- less vector-clean

## Drift sign
The image depends on pixel-art meme lineage.

---

# Not random internet nonsense

## What that is
Any weird online image with no coherent grammar.

## Why this style is different
`laser_cat_style` has:
- recurring motif families
- stable composition modes
- predictable humor engines
- consistent tone

## Drift sign
The image has no recognizable style structure.

# Bottom line

`laser_cat_style` is defined by what it is AND what it is not.

It is not:
- everything with cats
- everything cosmic
- everything colorful
- everything funny
- everything collage

It is:
- cat-centric collage
- with specific motifs
- in specific modes
- with specific tone
- and specific artifact preferences

Knowing what the style is not helps preserve what it is.
