# Black Void Stage

The black void is a powerful minimalist background that makes foreground elements pop.

## Characteristics
- Pure or near-black
- No detail
- Maximum contrast
- Stage-like presentation
- Dramatic isolation

## Use cases
- Single-gag hero mode
- Giant cat scenes
- Beam judgment scenes
- Iconic compositions
- High-contrast drama

## Best practices
- Ensure cat/object has sufficient brightness
- Use glow effects for drama
- Maintain silhouette clarity
- Avoid losing dark-furred cats

## Variations
- Pure black
- Near-black with subtle stars
- Black with gradient edges
- Void with minimal cosmic dust

## Drift risks
- Too empty (under-escalated)
- Cat too dark (lost subject)
- Boring without effects
