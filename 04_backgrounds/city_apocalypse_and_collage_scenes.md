# City Apocalypse and Collage Scenes

City and apocalypse backgrounds provide dramatic stakes and scale reference for giant cat scenes.

## Characteristics
- Urban skylines
- Grayscale or muted colors
- Dramatic clouds
- Destruction suggestion
- Scale reference

## Use cases
- Giant cat attack
- Kaiju cat scenes
- Beam judgment
- God-cat mode
- Threat tableau

## Best practices
- Grayscale city with colorful cat (contrast)
- Dramatic but not too detailed
- Scale contrast obvious
- Vulnerable atmosphere

## Variations
- Daytime city
- Night city
- Grayscale collage
- Apocalypse clouds
- Beach invasion

## Drift risks
- Too realistic (becomes serious kaiju)
- Too detailed (competes with cat)
- Too colorful (loses contrast opportunity)
